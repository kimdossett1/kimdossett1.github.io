CP Nassau's
Festival of Trees

By Kim Dossett and Joshua Ringler

Cerebral Palsy Nassau’s 28th Annual Festival of Trees took place from November 24th to 26th at the Cradle of Aviation in Garden City.

The event included live performances such as the Uniondale High School Band and various dance groups.

The event included live performances and vendors selling anything from hair accessories to holiday decorations to fresh honey.

While vendors are able to keep the profit from their sales, they pay a $450 fee that goes directly to CP Nassau.

Vendors included Department 56 as well as local businesses such as Miss Molly’s Honeydrippers.
